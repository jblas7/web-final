COOKIES POLICY

At PEC Burger, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and online visitors. This cookies policy describes how we use cookies and similar technologies when you use our website and related services.

1. What are cookies:

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help improve your browsing experience by remembering your preferences and previous visits.

2. Types of cookies we use:

    Essential cookies: These cookies are necessary for the basic functioning of our website. They enable basic functionalities like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

    Third-party cookies: We use third-party cookies to integrate external content, such as videos and social media, and to collect statistical and performance data. You can choose to accept or reject these cookies through your browser settings.

3. How we use cookies:

We use cookies for the following purposes:

    To ensure the proper functioning of our website.

    To remember your preferences and settings during and between visits.

    To improve the functionality and security of our website.

    To integrate third-party content, such as videos and social media.

    To collect statistical data about the use of our website.

4. Managing cookies:

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and set most browsers to prevent them from being stored. However, if you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site and some services and functionalities may not work.

5. Information security:

We take reasonable steps to protect the information collected through cookies from unauthorized access, use, alteration, or disclosure. However, no data transmission over the Internet or storage system is completely secure.

6. Changes to this cookies policy:

We reserve the right to change this cookies policy at any time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting them on our website or by other appropriate means. We encourage you to review this cookies policy periodically for any changes.
