Our History

At PEC Burger, our story is as rich and flavorful as our signature burgers. It all began with Juan Hernandez, a visionary entrepreneur with a deep-seated passion for culinary excellence and a love for the humble burger.

Juan's journey into the world of gastronomy started in his childhood kitchen, where he developed a keen appreciation for the art of cooking. Inspired by the sights, sounds, and aromas of his family's meals, Juan dreamed of one day creating dishes that would not only tantalize the taste buds but also warm the hearts of those who enjoyed them.

As Juan grew older, his passion for cooking flourished, leading him to pursue formal training in the culinary arts. Armed with a repertoire of skills and an insatiable hunger for knowledge, Juan embarked on a journey of discovery, exploring different cuisines, techniques, and flavor profiles.

It was during this culinary odyssey that Juan's love for burgers truly blossomed. Drawn to the versatility and endless possibilities of this beloved comfort food, Juan set out to reinvent the traditional burger experience, infusing it with his own unique flair and creativity.

With a clear vision in mind, Juan founded PEC Burger, a culinary haven where burgers were not just a meal but an experience. From the very beginning, Juan's commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability set PEC Burger apart, earning the admiration and loyalty of burger enthusiasts across Zaragoza and Madrid.

Today, PEC Burger stands as a testament to Juan's unwavering dedication to his craft and his relentless pursuit of culinary perfection. With two thriving locations and a legion of loyal fans, PEC Burger continues to push the boundaries of burger excellence, inspiring others to embrace their passion and follow their dreams.

Join us at PEC Burger and become a part of our story. From our kitchen to your plate, we invite you to experience the magic of our burgers and join us on a journey of flavor, innovation, and community. Welcome to PEC Burger, where every bite tells a story.
